國建會的使命 (Mission of MCAPA)

我們致力於促進台灣與美國在科技與學術領域的交流與合作，通過多樣化的活動和研討會，為專業人士、學者和學生提供一個開放、無政治色彩的平台。我們強調知識分享與跨領域合作，推動雙方在科技創新和學術發展上的共同進步。我們的願景是通過這些合作，增進台美關係，促進社會進步與未來發展。

We are dedicated to fostering collaboration and exchanges between Taiwan and the United States in technology and academia. Through diverse events and seminars, we provide an open, non-political platform for professionals, scholars, and students. We emphasize knowledge sharing and cross-disciplinary cooperation to drive joint progress in technological innovation and academic development. Our vision is to strengthen Taiwan-U.S. relations and promote societal progress and future growth through these collaborations.
